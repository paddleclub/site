Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What We Collect

When you submit a datapoint, we collect the information you provide:

  • Name and email address
  • City, state, and ZIP code
  • Spend amount and category

We may also collect basic usage data (page visits, browser type) through standard web analytics.

How We Use It

Your data is used to build anonymized, aggregate advocacy reports that demonstrate pickleball's economic impact to local governments. Individual submissions are never identified in any public report or presentation.

We may use your contact information to send updates about the campaign in your area, new programs, or initiatives relevant to pickleball infrastructure. You can opt out at any time.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share information with trusted service providers and program partners who help us operate PaddleClub or facilitate programs you choose to participate in. Any such sharing is limited to what is necessary to deliver those services or programs.

If you opt into a specific program, promotion, or partnership offered through PaddleClub, we may share relevant information with the applicable partner to fulfill your participation in that program.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. To do so, email troy@paddleclub.org.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at troy@paddleclub.org.
